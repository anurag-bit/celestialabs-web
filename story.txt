Logo:
CELESTIAL LABS // IDENTITY SIGNAL

THE CORE PHILOSOPHY The logo must not look like a "tech startup" (no gradients, no abstract blobs). It must look like a SEAL. A stamp of heavy industry and sovereign authority. It should feel like it could be etched into titanium or printed on a bond certificate.

THE MARK: "THE EVENT HORIZON"
Visual: A perfect, solid circle of Void Black (#050505). Surrounded by an extremely thin, high-contrast ring of Starlight White (#F5F5F7). The ring is not uniform; it is slightly thicker at the bottom-left and thins out to a razor edge at the top-right.

Meaning:

The Eclipse: The moment of alignment.
The Shield: Protection and durability.
The Lens: Focus and observation (Research).
Ascension: The thinning line points upward and outward.
THE TYPOGRAPHY: "THE INSTITUTION"
Font: Inter (Medium Weight) or IBM Plex Mono (for the 'LABS' part). Tracking: Wide (0.15em). Alignment: The text "CELESTIAL LABS" sits adjacent to the mark, or below it. The height of the text block exactly matches the diameter of the mark.

Color: Primary: Platinum (#E5E4E2). It should look silver, not white.

APPLICATION
On White Paper: The mark is pure black. Sharp.
On Dark Screens: The mark is an outline of light. A ghost.
Favicon: The Ring.
ANIMATION (WEB)
The ring draws itself slowly, starting from the heavy bottom and whipping around to the thin top. The center remains absolute void.
